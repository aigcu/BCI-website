Our mission

This cross-discipline project aims to explore, build, and test a brain-computer interface (BCI) product that can be added to an existing power wheelchair, allowing highly disabled patients with little or no limb movement to control the wheelchair. The development of this technology could significantly impact the healthcare industry and the lives of those confined to the boundary of their bodies by providing them with a more intuitive and independent means of mobility.
